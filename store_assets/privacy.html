HiteZero — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026 · Developer: GGHF

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game HiteZero (“the Game”, package com.gghf.hitezero) handles your information.

1. Information we collect

HiteZero does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The Game runs locally on your device. It does not require an account and does not gather your name, email address, location, contacts, or advertising/device identifiers.

2. Device permissions

The Game requests the Vibration permission only to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. This permission does not access or transmit any data.

3. Third-party services

The Game does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

4. Children’s privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any information from children and is suitable for general audiences.

5. Data storage

Your game progress and settings are stored only on your own device. Uninstalling the Game removes this local data.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at team.gghf@gmail.com.
